The Employee Retention Tax Credit

Maximizing Your Claims For Keeping Americans Employed
The government has authorized unprecedented stimulus, and yet billions of dollars will go unclaimed.

Funded by the CARES Act

Originally created to encourage business to keep employees on the payroll as they navigate the unprecedented effect of COVID-19

The ERTC was established by the Coronavirus Aid, Relief and Economic Security (CARES) Act.

It provides a credit against qualified wages and health plan expenses paid from 2020 Q2 thru 2021 Q3 or Q4.

It is available even for businesses that had PPP loans.

No Restrictions - No Repayment

This is not a loan - you can share it with your employees, expand your business or add it to your retirement nest egg.

While the ERTC was created in the CARES act along with the PPP Loans - this is not a loan.

THERE IS NO REPAYMENT!!

There are no restrictions on what recipients can do with the funds.

Up to $26,000 or $33,000 per W-2 Employee

Full time and part time Employees Qualify

The 2020 ERC Program is a tax credit of up to:

  • in 2020, $5k per employee for pre-existing businesses
  • in 2021, $26k per employee for pre-existing businesses
  • in 2021, $33k per employee for businesses started after 2/15/2020
